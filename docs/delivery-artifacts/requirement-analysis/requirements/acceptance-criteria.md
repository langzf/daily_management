Traceability IDs: REQ-001, REQ-002
Status: draft

### REQ-001
Given: A clear business goal and measurable KPI are provided.
When: Requirement baseline is reviewed.
Then: The requirement is unambiguous and testable.

### REQ-002
Given: User scenario and constraints are documented.
When: Requirement specification is reviewed.
Then: Engineering can implement without scope ambiguity.
